Privacy Policy — FoodScanner

Last updated: March 2026

1. Overview

FoodScanner is an informational app that helps users identify food additives by scanning product barcodes. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

2. Data We Collect

FoodScanner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. The app only stores your language preference and terms acceptance locally on your device.

3. Camera Permission

The app requests access to your device camera solely for the purpose of scanning product barcodes. No images or video are stored or transmitted.

4. Third-Party Services

Open Food Facts: Product data is retrieved from the Open Food Facts public API (world.openfoodfacts.org). No personal data is sent to this service.

Google AdMob: The app displays non-personalized advertisements via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect the Advertising ID of your device. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage

The only data stored locally on your device is your selected language and confirmation that you have accepted the terms of use. This data never leaves your device.

6. Children

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: yourfoodscanner@gmail.com
